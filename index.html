Reading Insights
Aug 2, 2025
"DOK vs AI: Exploring the Value of Student Learning in the Age of AI" - Greg Sullivan
This article sums up pretty neatly how important it is for AI usage in the classroom to be addressed in a meaningful way. The longer we wait, the harder it will be to adapt teaching methods to effectively integrate AI tools while still promoting deep learning and critical thinking skills among students. It is so critical that students learn the foundations of a subject organically, and not just rely on AI to do the "thinking" for them.
Jul 28, 2025
"Attention Is All You Need" - Transformer Architecture
Reading this paper helped me understand why transformers revolutionized NLP. The self-attention mechanism is elegant in its simplicity, yet powerful enough to capture long-range dependencies that RNNs struggled with. It's fascinating how removing recurrence actually improved sequential modeling.
